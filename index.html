Gib Goy Games

"Thinking toys for big birls and goys."

"'Gib Goy' is 'Big Boy' spelled sideways. Sort of." -- Bill St. Clair, proprietor

Our first product, Kakuro Dojo, is available for iOS and Android.

  

Spokes is a board game, invented by my son, Chris St. Clair. Click the "Help" and "Rules" links above and below the board to learn how to play.

Archmage is a simpler board game that Chris invented. Easier to learn, but still plenty interesting. Click the "Help" and "Rules" links above and below the board to learn how to play.

Zephyrnot is another of Chris's inventions. Click the "Instructions" and "Rules" links below the board to learn how to play. Networked play in development.

JSMaze is a simple first-person maze game, patterned after a game I played at MIT in the late 1970s, on the PDP-10 and Imlac terminals at Project Mac. Still in development. Networked play and chat coming.

Zippy is a little hack that makes images of Zippy the Pinhead, Milo, and Mr. Natural bounce around the screen. Silly and funny, with real elastic collision simulation.
